We can change title of your Angular project from Src/index.html

We can change full content of page that is default, from Src/app/app.component.html

How to use title of your page in main component page,app.component.html,simply we declare Interpolation(nested curely braces) and inside it declare title.

Note:- 1.Our Angular title, we seen it src/index.html,that means we change our Angular website title from that file. 2.Our Angular full Content is available in Src/app/app.component.html,You can set in this file heading,paragraph,div,section and all. 3.In Src/app/app.component.ts your angular application title property is their,so you display your title in app.component.html like:- Welcome to Angulr{{title}} In this file,we write our all typescript logic In this Example Welcome to Angular coming from app.component.html and title coming from app.component.ts
