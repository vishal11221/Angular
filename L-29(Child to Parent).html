Interview-Question:- 1.How to transfer data from Parentcomponent to Childcomponent? 2.How to transfer data from Childcomponent to Parentcomponent ?

Angular uses @Input() and @Output() to transfer data between the parent context and child directives or components. Data enters a component via its input attributes. The terms output refer to how data flows out of a component via its output characteristics. As a result, the terms input and output were coined.

3.When we use @Input() and @Output? When the data transfer Parent to Child then we use @Input(). And When the data transfer Child to Parent then we use @Output().
